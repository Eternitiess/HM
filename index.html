Unit 6: Hospitality Human Resource Management

HRM is essential for acquiring, training, appraising, and compensating employees while managing relations, safety, and fairness at work.

  • Key Functions: Planning, Organizing, Staffing, Leading, Controlling
  • Importance: Proper HR management avoids hiring mistakes, reduces turnover, and enhances employee performance.

Unit 7: Marketing for the Hospitality Industry

Focus on creating and capturing customer value, attracting new customers, and retaining current ones through satisfaction and loyalty programs.

  • Key Marketing Trends: Sustainability, Digital Marketing, Service Employees' Role

Unit 8: Managing Safety and Security Issues

Safety and security are key to protecting guests and employees. Emergency planning and legal compliance are essential for minimizing risks.

Unit 9: Financial Management in Hospitality

Understanding revenue, costs, and budgeting is crucial for profitability. Cost management and revenue maximization are key aspects.

Unit 10: Delivering Hospitality Services

Ensuring that visitor experiences meet expectations by delivering high-quality services and empowering employees for better customer satisfaction.

Unit 11: Managing Hospitality Technology

Implementing digital tools such as PMS and CRM to enhance customer experience and operational efficiency while ensuring security.

Unit 12: Conceiving Hospitality Processes

Designing service processes to meet performance objectives like speed, quality, and flexibility using service blueprinting.

Unit 13: Designing Hospitality Processes

Designing touchpoints and service interactions that create positive guest experiences while ensuring operational efficiency.

Unit 14: Delivering Hospitality Services

Focuses on guest satisfaction, quality management, and empowering frontline employees to deliver excellent service.
